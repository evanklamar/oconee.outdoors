This kayaking excursion will start at the south shore of Blue Ridge Lake, where we will paddle down the Toccoa River until we reach the pickup spot near State Highway 60 east of Aska. The excursion group will meet at the Lake Blue Ridge Day Use Area at 9 AM, though we are looking for people to help transport the kayaks.
